Ashmita Bhattacharyya

I am a fourth-year Engineering Science student at the University of Toronto, majoring in Machine Intelligence, with a certificate in Engineering Business.

Currently, I am working as a Machine Learning Research Intern at Huawei on the LLM Reasoning team, developing transformer-based architectures for ARC-AGI benchmarks.

Prior to this, I was an Undergraduate Researcher at the Vector Institute in CleverHans Lab, working on differentially private ML, supervised by Prof. Nicolas Papernot.

I had also spent a summer at the National University of Singapore, working on an applied ML project on optimizing perovskite solar cell design, in collaboration with the Solar Energy Research Institute of Singapore (SERIS).

Growing up, I travelled a lot. I spent most of my childhood in Singapore, a couple years in Dubai, and moved to Montréal in High School. Outside of work, I enjoy the gym, visual arts, good coffee, and long outdoor walks.
